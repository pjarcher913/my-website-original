CSE Capstone

My Arizona State University Computer Systems Engineering Capstone Project was sponsored by Benchmark Electronics. Myself and 3 other students were tasked with developing a system with the ability to provide real-time 3-dimensional locations of individual on-scene first responders without the use of GPS or an internet connection. Our final prototype utilized wirelessly-interconnected sub-systems of microcontrollers and sensors to operate. All code was written in embedded C/C++, Python, SQLite, and Linux shell.

CSE335 Final Project

CSE335 is an iOS Application Development course that I took in my senior year. For my final project, I developed an iOS app that located bathrooms near a user's current position and displayed their pinned locations on an interactive map. The app utilized GoogleMaps APIs and CoreLocation to determine the locations of the user and nearby bathrooms. I wanted to integrate a FireBase system, but I ended up not having enough time.
